This directory contains dictionary files and other content that is used by the semantics module of cider.
The directories may be empty in the release file.
Content for the semantics modules may be retrieved either online by the module itself (if available)
or some dictionaries may be also not public available and must be retrieved in some other way. This may
apply to some commercial dictionaries that cannot be attached to the free software part from repository.

notes:
the following knowledge bases could be useful:
http://www.freebase.com  (community collaboration, 2.5 million statements) 
http://www.opencyc.org, http://sw.opencyc.org/downloads/opencyc_owl_downloads_v2/opencyc-latest.owl.gz (60.000 statements)
http://www.dbpedia.org (extraction from wikipedia, 270 million statements)
http://mpii.de/yago (extraction from wikipedia, 20 million statements)
http://www.musicbrainz.org (artists, songs, albums 23.000 statements)
http://www.geonames.org (countries, cities, capitals, 85.000 statements)
http://www4.wiwiss.fu-berlin.de/dblp/ (papers, authors, citations, 15 million statements)
http://www.rdfabout.com/demo/census/ (population statistics, 1000 million statements)
